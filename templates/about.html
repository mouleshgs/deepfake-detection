About Us

Welcome to the Deepfake Image Detector. This application uses a machine learning model to detect if an image is real or deepfake.

The model is based on the VGG16 architecture, fine-tuned for binary classification of images. It leverages deep learning techniques and image preprocessing to determine whether an image is manipulated.

Our goal is to help identify and combat the spread of deepfake content in social media and other platforms.
